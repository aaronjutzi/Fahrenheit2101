Methodology: How we did it

Preparing the study area:

Data pre-processing and setting up an extract, transfer, and load workflow (ETL) was the most arduous task undertaken in this project. Since the data pre-processing and the ETL processes are intertwined with each other they were included in the same methodology section here. Weather station location data for all of Canada was acquired from a Government of Canada website and then preprocessed in QGIS. Weather stations within Ontario and within a 50km distance from the borders of Ontario were clipped for inclusion in the study area. The weather stations within 50km outside of the Ontario border were included in the study area to improve interpolation results near the edges of Ontario near Manitoba and Quebec.
